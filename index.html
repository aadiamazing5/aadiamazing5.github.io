First Robotics

I am an alumni of Ottawa's First Robotics Team (FRC) called Merge Robotics Team 2706. The team has over 60 members which are divided into subteams. As a member of the Software subteam, I helped create the code that operated the robot. For the 2019-2020 Infinite Recharge Season, we created a robot named High Ground. Along with a small team, I worked on the intake subsytem that took in balls, and fed them to the shooter. Using technology/software including Java and WPILib, I spent most of the year working and learning on this project. FRC has helped me learn new skills, refine my communication and teamwork skills, and has made me a much stronger software developer.
