Random Bot

  • Category: Discord Bot

  • Project date: July 2020

  • Project URL: https://github.com/aadiamazing5/RandomBot

Made with Discords python library (discord.py) this bot leverages the powerful Python random library to bring an easy-to-use and accesible randomizer bot to Discord, a popular messaging and voice call application. The bot is hosted on Heroku to ensure 24/7 access to its features. The bot has been added to over 100 servers with a total of 800+ users, and as a result, has been verified by Discord. This project helped develop my mastery of python and its libraries, and allowed me to practice working with frameworks like discord.py.
