I’ve created hundreds of designs using Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator over five years, and even ran a profitable business when I was younger where I created logos, banners and stream overlays for Youtube content creators. My most notable project was when I was recruited to create a logo for the Developer Operations (DevOps) team at Innovapost, Canadapost’s IT division. The logo can be seen above, and is still used by the company. This project was my my first venture into freelance graphic design, and was an excellent way for me to further refine my Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator skills.
